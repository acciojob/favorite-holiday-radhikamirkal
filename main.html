Paris, France


Paris is one of the most beautiful and romantic cities in the world, famous for its art, culture, architecture, fashion, history, and delicious cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic monument of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to world-famous artworks including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A beautiful avenue for shopping and sightseeing.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A magical experience especially at night.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery, and delicious.
  • Baguette – Freshly baked and crispy.
  • Macarons – Colorful and sweet French desserts.
  • Crêpes – Soft, tasty, and filled with chocolate or fruits.
  • French Cheese – Rich, creamy, and full of flavor.
