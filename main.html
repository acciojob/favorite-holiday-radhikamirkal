Paris, France


Paris is a beautiful and romantic city known for art, culture, architecture, and cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – Iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – Gothic masterpiece.
  4. Champs-Élysées – Famous shopping avenue.
  5. Seine River Cruise – Magical night views.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Buttery and flaky.
  • Baguette – Fresh and crispy.
  • Macarons – Colorful sweet treats.
  • Crêpes – Soft and delicious.
  • French Cheese – Rich and flavorful.
